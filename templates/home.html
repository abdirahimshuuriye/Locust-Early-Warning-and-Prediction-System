System Features

🤖

Machine Learning Prediction

Predicts locust outbreak risk using rainfall, temperature, and soil moisture data.

🗺️

Risk Map Monitoring

Displays regional locust risk status across Somalia using clear visual risk maps.

📧

Email Alert System

Sends automatic warning emails to registered farmers when high-risk conditions are detected.

📊

Reports and Analytics

Provides dashboards, charts, reports, and export tools for better decision-making.

Why Early Warning Matters

Locust swarms can destroy crops and vegetation within a short time. Early prediction helps farmers, organizations, and authorities respond before serious damage occurs.

Supporting Farmers

When a high-risk outbreak is detected, the system sends email alerts to registered farmers so they can monitor their farms and prepare early control actions.
